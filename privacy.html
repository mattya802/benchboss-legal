Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2024

Overview

Bench Boss ("we", "our", or "the app") is a youth sports lineup management application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

The app allows you to create teams, player rosters, and game lineups. This data includes:

  • Team names
  • Player names (first names or nicknames you choose to enter)
  • Game schedules and lineup configurations

Important: All of this data is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your team or player data on external servers. We have no access to this information.

Advertising

The free version of the app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect certain information for advertising purposes, including:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region)
  • App usage data

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. All transactions are handled by Apple or Google according to their respective privacy policies.

How We Use Information

Since all app data is stored locally on your device, we do not use your personal information for any purpose. The app functions entirely offline.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Advertising Partners: Google AdMob may collect data as described above for advertising purposes (free version only).
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law.

Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and protected by your device's security features. We recommend keeping your device locked and updated to protect your data.

Children's Privacy

The app is designed for coaches managing youth sports teams. While coaches may enter player names, we encourage using first names only or nicknames. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. All data remains on the coach's device.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains on your device until you delete it within the app or uninstall the app. We have no access to your data and cannot delete it remotely.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information. You can:

  • View all your data within the app
  • Edit or delete any team, player, or game data
  • Delete all data by uninstalling the app

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

support@benchboss.app
